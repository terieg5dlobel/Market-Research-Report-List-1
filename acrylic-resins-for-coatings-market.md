<p><p>Acrylic resins for coatings are primarily classified into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based acrylic coatings are eco-friendlier, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), quick drying times, and easy cleanup with water, making them ideal for indoor applications. In contrast, solvent-based acrylic coatings provide enhanced durability and adhesion, suitable for external surfaces and harsh environments. Both types are formulated for specific applications, balancing performance, environmental considerations, and application methods to meet diverse market needs.</p></p>
