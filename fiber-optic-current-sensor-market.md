<p><p>Fiber optic current sensors are categorized into two main types: DC and AC. DC type sensors are designed to measure direct current by detecting the magnetic fields generated by the current flow, offering high accuracy and stability. AC type sensors, on the other hand, are used for alternating current measurements, leveraging the change in magnetic fields over time. Both types utilize fiber optic technology to enhance sensitivity and provide electrical isolation, making them suitable for various industrial and utility applications.</p></p>
