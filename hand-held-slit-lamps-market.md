<p><p>The hand-held slit lamp market is primarily divided into two types: monocular and binocular hand-held slit lamps. Monocular hand-held slit lamps feature a single ocular lens, offering a compact and portable option for basic eye examinations. They are ideal for quick assessments in various settings. In contrast, binocular hand-held slit lamps provide enhanced depth perception and a wider field of view by utilizing two ocular lenses. This type is better suited for detailed examinations and diagnostics, appealing to specialized practices.</p></p>
