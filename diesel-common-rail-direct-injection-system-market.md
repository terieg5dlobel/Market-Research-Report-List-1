<p><ul><li>Solenoid Type</li><li>Piezo Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDi) system market is primarily divided into two types: solenoid type and piezo type injectors. Solenoid type injectors use electromagnetic solenoids to control fuel injection, offering reliable performance and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in various vehicle applications. In contrast, piezo type injectors utilize piezoelectric crystals for faster and more precise fuel delivery, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for advanced diesel engines in the automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
