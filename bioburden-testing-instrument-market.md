<p><p>The bioburden testing instrument market features key methods such as the Membrane Filtration Method and Plate Count Method. The Membrane Filtration Method involves passing samples through a filter that captures microorganisms, allowing for quantitative analysis. In contrast, the Plate Count Method entails inoculating a growth medium with the sample and incubating it to enumerate viable organisms. Both methods are integral for assessing microbial contamination in pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and cosmetics, ensuring product safety and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
