<p><p>The OLED encapsulation system market primarily comprises two types: TFE (Transparent Flexible Encapsulation) and Hybrid encapsulation. TFE utilizes a flexible barrier layer, providing excellent moisture and oxygen protection, crucial for enhancing OLED longevity. In contrast, Hybrid systems combine organic and inorganic materials, offering a balance of flexibility and durability while optimizing performance for varied applications. Both types aim to improve the stability and efficiency of OLED displays, catering to the growing demand in consumer electronics and other industries.</p></p>
